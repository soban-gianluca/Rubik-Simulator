## 🎮 Gameplay Features

### **Solve Assistance & Learning**
- **Step-by-step tutorial mode** - Guided lessons for beginners (F2L, OLL, PLL algorithms)
- **Hint system** - Show next optimal move or highlight pieces that need attention
- **Algorithm trainer** - Practice specific algorithms with visual guides
- **Solve detection** - Automatically detect and name algorithms being performed
- **Undo/Redo with branching** - Tree-like history allowing multiple solution paths

### **Game Modes & Challenges**
- **Speedsolving mode** - Official WCA timer with inspection time
- **Relay races** - Solve multiple cube sizes (2x2, 3x3, 4x4, 5x5)
- **Pattern challenges** - Create specific patterns (checkerboard, cross, etc.)
- **Blindfolded mode** - Memorization phase + execution phase
- **One-handed mode** - Restricted controls simulation
- **Move limit puzzles** - Solve in X moves or less
- **Daily challenges** - New puzzle every day with leaderboards

### **Cube Customization**
- **Multiple cube sizes** - 2x2, 4x4, 5x5, even larger cubes
- **Custom color schemes** - Neon, pastel, high-contrast, colorblind-friendly
- **Texture packs** - Wood, metal, carbon fiber, transparent, stickers vs. plastic
- **Cube brands simulation** - Different corner cutting, feel, and sound per brand
- **Wear and aging effects** - Simulate used cube appearance

### **Advanced Rendering**
- **Realistic lighting & shadows** - Dynamic lighting with multiple sources
- **Particle effects** - Celebration effects when solved, move trails
- **Motion blur** - During fast rotations for realism
- **Reflection mapping** - Glossy cube surfaces reflecting environment
- **Post-processing effects** - Bloom, depth of field, ambient occlusion

### **Environment & Atmosphere**
- **Interactive environments** - Competition halls, study rooms, outdoor scenes
- **Dynamic weather** - Rain effects on windows, changing time of day
- **Multiple camera angles** - Close-up, isometric, first-person solving view
- **VR support** - Full immersion with hand tracking
- **AR mode** - Solve virtual cubes on real tables via camera

### **Performance & Optimization**
- **Level-of-detail (LOD)** - Reduce geometry when far from camera
- **Instanced rendering** - Optimize multiple cube rendering
- **Threaded animation** - Smooth animations even during heavy computation
- **Adaptive quality** - Auto-adjust graphics based on performance

### **Advanced Animation System**
- **Realistic physics** - Cube pieces have weight and momentum
- **Corner cutting simulation** - Realistic movement based on cube mechanism
- **Multi-layer rotations** - Wide turns (Rw, Uw, etc.)
- **Slice moves** - M, E, S moves with proper animation
- **Cube rotations** - x, y, z rotations of entire cube
